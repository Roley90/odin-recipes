Pizza recipe

Description

Pizza is everyone's favorite dish. Make your own masterpiece with the help of this simple recipe!

Ingredients

  • 30dkg flour
  • 1g salt
  • 15dkg mozarella
  • tomato extraction
  • serrano ham
  • onion

Steps

  1. Make a nice pizza pasta with the help of this recipe
  2. Bake it at 240C for 15 minutes
  3. Put the tomato powder on it and then place the rest of the Ingredients in the order you like
  4. Bake it another 10 minutes
  5. Ready to eat!
